5 Signs You’re Not Ready for a Pet

  • You Thought Cats Just Watch Netflix: If your idea of owning a cat is sharing snacks while binge-watching TV, you’re in for a surprise. Cats don’t care about your shows—they care about knocking over your popcorn bowl.
  • “Daily Walks? I Didn’t Sign Up for This!”: Owning a dog isn’t just about Instagram photos. Daily walks mean rain, snow, and the occasional embarrassing poop bag mishap.
  • “Do Pets Come with an App?”: If you’ve ever caught yourself wondering why your pet doesn’t respond to a push notification, it might be time to rethink your readiness for a real-life, non-digital companion.
  • "Your Dog’s Fitness Tracker Has Better Stats Than Yours" Pets need exercise, and if walking your dog leaves you winded - it's safe to say that you guys don't deserve eachother.
  • "Your Budget Doesn’t Include “Chewed Couch Repairs”: Owning a pet isn’t cheap. From surprise vet bills to furniture replacements, your wallet will be yelling for help.
